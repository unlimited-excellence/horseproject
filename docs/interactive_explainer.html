The HORSE Project

An interactive ecosystem where intellectual achievement is transformed into a tangible, digital asset through the "Learn-to-Earn" model.

The Problem: Undervalued Intellectual Capital

In our modern world, immense intellectual effort in fields like programming, mathematics, and science often goes without direct material reward. Traditional accolades like diplomas and medals don't capture the economic value created. This gap leads to dampened motivation and inefficient investment in the most valuable resource: human intellect.

The Solution: A Learn-to-Earn Ecosystem

HORSE introduces a self-regulating economy that bridges this gap. By tokenizing success in verifiable intellectual competitions, we create a direct financial incentive for learning and a new asset class for investors. Explore the mechanics of our ecosystem below.

The Dual-Token Economy

The ecosystem operates on two core assets: the HORSE utility token and tradable Talent Shares (SHR). This separation allows for both a fluid reward mechanism and a stable investment framework.

HORSE Token

The primary utility and reward token of the ecosystem.

  • Function: Earned for competitive success, used for governance (DAO votes), and provides liquidity on Decentralized Exchanges (DEX).
  • Supply: Unlimited, but its issuance is strictly tied to real, verifiable achievements, ensuring value is backed by intellectual work.
  • Principle: "Fair Launch" - no pre-mining or ICO, guaranteeing tokens are earned through merit.

Talent Shares (SHR)

An NFT-based investment vehicle representing a stake in a participant's future success.

  • Function: Grants holders the right to receive dividends (70% of earned HORSE rewards). Tradable on the internal Intellectual Exchange.
  • Supply: Fixed at 1,000 unique shares per participant, creating a clear investment structure.
  • Principle: Allows participants to gain upfront funding and enables investors to sponsor and profit from promising talent.

Community Governance via DAO

The HORSE project is designed for long-term decentralization. Key decisions are handed over to the community through a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), where HORSE token holders vote on the ecosystem's future.

Voting Power

Every HORSE token represents a vote, ensuring that the most invested community members have a proportional say in the project's direction. This aligns control with long-term commitment.

Key Governable Parameters

The DAO's primary role is to ensure the economic stability and strategic growth of the ecosystem. Token holders can propose and vote on changes to:

  • Economic Stability: Adjustments to the reward formula's parameters (like coefficient K or the cubic exponent) to manage token issuance and value.
  • Ecosystem Expansion: Prioritizing which new intellectual platforms (e.g., LeetCode, Kaggle, Math Olympiads) to integrate next.
  • Treasury Allocation: Directing community funds towards development grants, marketing initiatives, or bug bounties.
  • Protocol Upgrades: Approving updates to the smart contracts and core infrastructure.
